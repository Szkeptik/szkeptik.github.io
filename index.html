About Us

Performance marketing meets sharp design

Harmony Lab is a boutique digital agency based in Pécs, Hungary, specialising in paid media management and web design. We combine data-driven campaign strategy with conversion-focused design to deliver measurable growth for our clients.

Our expertise spans PPC & Paid Media — managing high-performance campaigns across Google Ads, Meta Ads, TikTok, and Microsoft Advertising — and Website & Landing Page design built to convert the traffic we drive.

With 8 years of Project Management experience in multinational R&D, we bring the same rigour to every client engagement — structured planning, budget discipline, and clear communication. Managing a campaign portfolio is no different from managing a project pipeline: both demand clear targets, fast iteration, and accountability for results.

We design and build websites from layout and UX to interactive JavaScript elements. A well-designed landing page is part of the campaign: it's where ad spend either converts or dies.

Landing Page Design

Pages built to convert

A paid campaign is only as strong as the page it leads to. At Harmony Lab, landing page design isn't an afterthought — it's a core part of every campaign we run. We design, build, and continuously optimise pages with a single goal: turning clicks into leads.

Our landing pages are built around conversion rate optimisation (CRO) principles from the ground up — clear value propositions, frictionless user journeys, trust signals, and CTAs placed with intent. Every element earns its position by serving the conversion goal.

We treat CTR (click-through rate) and CTA performance as live metrics, not launch-day checkboxes. Through iterative A/B testing of headlines, button copy, page layouts, and form designs, we consistently push conversion rates higher over the lifecycle of a campaign.

⚡
Speed & Core Web Vitals
Fast-loading pages reduce bounce and improve Quality Score — directly lowering your cost per click.
🎯

Ad copy and landing page content aligned precisely — visitors land on exactly what was promised, reducing drop-off.
